F.A.Q

  • What is CoinAppIOS?

    A script that automates watching ads and completing minigames (and more to come) in order to earn 100% more coins through COIN.

  • How do i install?

    1. Add repo to your package manager "https://coinappios.github.io"

    2. Install "coinappios" (This will also install Offical AutoTouch and Curl)

    3. Get an AutoTouch license (refer to "Do i need an AutoTouch License?")

    4. Open IOS settings and change AutoLock to never.

    5. Hold down the volume down button and select "COINAPP vx.x.x.ate"

  • Do i need to change settings in the coinapp?

    Yes!

    1. Tap profile at top left and open settings

    2. Turn off reward surveys.

  • Where can i get support?

    You can join our telegram community group where our helpful members and moderators can assist you.

  • Do i need an AutoTouch License?

    We do not support cracked versions. The bot script runs on AutoTouch, to obtain an autotouch license:
    1. Open AutoTouch app.

    2. Go to AutoTouch settings.

    3. Go to license.

    4. Purchase a license (Basic $9.99)

    5. Press Download license at top right.
